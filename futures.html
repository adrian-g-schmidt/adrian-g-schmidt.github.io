Futures Spinner

Futures Spinner

Role: Design and Fabrication

Description: The futures spinner is a prop I designed, built, and used for Futures workshops we run at the School of Cybernetics. Based off of the “Thing from the Future” card game, it is an acrylic box with three separate sections. Each section contains ping pong balls with different prompts on them. The box is spun, and a ball is pulled out from each section to generate a prompt for a futuring exercise.
