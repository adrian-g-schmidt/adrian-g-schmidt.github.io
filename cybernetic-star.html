Cybernetic Star Coasters

Role: Design and Fabrication

Description: The Cybernetic Star is an analytical tool created to simplify the process of thinking of interactions and dynamics within a complex system by pairing different categories. For a series of workshops, I was tasked with creating a version the 80 participants could take home with them. I designed, prototyped, and built these cybernetic star coasters, with an inner section that spins separately from the outside. They are made out of three layers of laser engraved and cut ply.
