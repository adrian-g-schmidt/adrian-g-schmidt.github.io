Backwash

Backwash

Role: Web development

Description: I developed the homepage of the Backwash website. Backwash is a T-Shirt company a few friends started. They wanted a home page that would track the cursor of the user while they were browsing. After experimenting with different 3D options, I chose to use 3 flat layers, that give the illusion of 3D pixelated eyeballs following the cursor as it moves around the page.
